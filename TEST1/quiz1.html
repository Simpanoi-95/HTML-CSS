Many young people are home during this time. How might they navigate this time and what can they engage themselves in?

Since the beginning of covid-19, a lot of institutions closed down. The group of young people at home are school going young people from primary to university and the other group is the unemployed youths due to termination from their previous places of work. Each of this groups got a chance to navigate through this hard times and make the best out of covid-19 situation.
School going young people can navigate this hard times by:

  • Follow simple health rules: Properly wash your hands more often, avoid crowded spaces, avoid handshaking, if you are sick wear a mask and seek medical attention immediately.
  • Educate your families, friends and community: Spread community awareness online and offline. many young people have since raised awareness on covid-19 in Kenya either through music or poems .an example is a young girl who performed a poem when the deputy president attended an event in the Eastern part of country . So let the young people be responsible. Verify every news and information before sharing. Be a source of calm during this storm.
  • Volunteering and donations young people can help in their societies by offering this services as they can collect donations and distribute.
  • Focusing in their studies since they will still go back
  • Helping their parents for simple house chores at home they can at least lend a hand
For the formerly employed young people they can navigate by:
  • using social media and apps to spread accurate information
  • Innovating to lead access to healthcare services.
  • Delivering food and other services as volunteers and activists
  • Engaging lawmakers to provide the needed support for in citizens for example free testing and health cover.
  • Ensuring the vulnerable in the society are not left behind by raising voice.
  • Lastly they can engage themselves in self-employment or seek other employment activities and sustain themselves
